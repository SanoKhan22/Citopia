 🏺 Citopia: Egyptian Transport Empire - Theme Specification

## Visual Theme Elements

### Terrain Types
- **Desert Sand** - Light brown/golden tiles, limited movement speed
- **Nile River** - Blue water tiles, fast boat transport
- **Fertile Farmland** - Green tiles near river, high population
- **Rocky Mountains** - Brown/gray tiles, expensive to build through
- **Oases** - Green spots in desert, natural trading posts

### Cities & Structures
- **Cairo** (Capital) - Large pyramid icons, multiple districts
- **Alexandria** (Port City) - Mediterranean access, lighthouse
- **Thebes** (Ancient Capital) - Temple complexes, tourist destination
- **Aswan** (Southern Trading Post) - Granite quarries, dam
- **Memphis** (Industrial) - Pyramid construction materials
- **Siwa Oasis** (Remote) - Date palm exports, luxury goods

### Vehicles (Egyptian Style)
#### Land Transport
- **Donkey Caravans** - Slow, cheap, desert-capable
- **Camel Trains** - Medium speed, high desert efficiency
- **Horse Carts** - Fast on roads, expensive maintenance
- **Ancient Egyptian Chariots** - Luxury passenger transport
- **Modern Trucks** - High capacity, road-dependent

#### Water Transport
- **Felucca Boats** - Traditional Nile sailing boats
- **Cargo Barges** - Heavy freight on Nile
- **Steam Boats** - Faster passenger service
- **Container Ships** - Mediterranean/Red Sea routes

#### Air Transport (Late Game)
- **Hot Air Balloons** - Tourist flights over pyramids
- **Modern Aircraft** - International passengers/cargo

### Cargo Types
#### Traditional Egyptian Goods
- **Stone Blocks** - For pyramid/temple construction
- **Papyrus** - Ancient paper, high value
- **Gold** - Mined in southern regions
- **Spices** - Cinnamon, cardamom from trade routes
- **Dates & Figs** - Oasis agriculture
- **Cotton** - Nile Delta farming, textile export

#### Modern Goods
- **Oil** - Desert oil fields (late game)
- **Tourists** - Special passenger category
- **Archaeological Artifacts** - Museum deliveries
- **Solar Panels** - Modern desert infrastructure

## Cultural Integration

### Game Mechanics with Egyptian Flavor
- **Pharaoh's Contracts** - Government missions for pyramid construction
- **Nile Flood Season** - Seasonal changes affecting river transport
- **Sandstorm Events** - Weather that disrupts desert routes
- **Tourist Season** - Increased passenger demand to historical sites
- **Archaeological Discoveries** - Random events creating new cargo types

### Visual Assets Needed
- **Desert tileset** with Egyptian architectural elements
- **Pyramids, sphinxes, obelisks** as landmark buildings
- **Palm trees and desert vegetation**
- **Traditional Egyptian and modern vehicles**
- **Hieroglyphic-inspired UI elements**
- **Egyptian color palette**: Gold, turquoise, sandy brown, Nile blue

### Audio/Music Theme
- **Middle Eastern instrumental music**
- **Egyptian traditional instruments** (oud, qanun, ney)
- **Desert wind sounds, Nile water flows**
- **Market/bazaar ambient sounds**

## Technical Implementation Notes

### Map Generation
- **Nile River** as central feature running north-south
- **Desert terrain** as primary landscape
- **Oases** scattered throughout desert
- **Mountain ranges** in eastern/western borders
- **Mediterranean coast** in northern region

### Unique Game Rules
- **Desert movement penalty** for non-camel vehicles
- **River transport efficiency** for bulk cargo
- **Seasonal flooding** affects river routes
- **Tourism bonuses** near historical landmarks
- **Heat effects** on vehicle maintenance costs
